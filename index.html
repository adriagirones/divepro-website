FAQ

Questions we get
a lot.

Does DivePro work on mobile? +
Yes — DivePro is fully mobile-optimised with a native-style bottom navigation bar, slide-in drawer, and bottom-sheet modals. Your instructors can check their schedule, log dives, and update bookings right from their phone. Desktop stays unchanged with the full sidebar layout.
Can I have different staff see different things? +
Absolutely. DivePro has a full roles and permissions system. You can create custom roles (e.g. "Senior Instructor", "Cashier") and precisely control which tabs and actions each role can access. Admins see everything; receptionists might only see bookings and invoices.
What languages does DivePro support? +
DivePro supports 14 interface languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Thai, Japanese, Chinese, Portuguese, and more. The owner can control whether language switching is available to staff, and each user can set their own preferred language independently.
Can I manage multiple dive shops? +
Yes — the Multi-Site plan gives you an owner portal where you can oversee all your locations from a single view: check billing, enter any school's dashboard, manage subscriptions, and run cross-school reports. You can also organise shops into groups with their own sub-admins.
Is my data safe? How do I migrate from my current system? +
Your data is stored in a secure, encrypted Supabase database. Migrations are straightforward — most shops are fully running within a day. We can provide import templates for your existing bookings, students, and staff data. And if you ever want to leave (you won't), we'll give you a full export.
